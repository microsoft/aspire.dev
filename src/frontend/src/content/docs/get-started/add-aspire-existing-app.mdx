When you add Aspire to an existing application, the first choice is the language of the AppHost. Your services can stay in their current languages while the AppHost handles orchestration, service discovery, configuration injection, and local startup.

<Aside type="note" title="Your app language and AppHost language are separate choices">
Choose the AppHost language that matches how you want to author the orchestration layer. Then, within that guide, choose the app stack you're bringing to Aspire.
</Aside>
