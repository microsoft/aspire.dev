Build your first Aspire app by first choosing the AppHost language you want to work in. The AppHost is the code-first orchestrator for your solution, so this choice determines whether your walkthrough uses `AppHost.cs` / `apphost.cs` or `apphost.ts`.

<Aside type="note" title="Start with the AppHost language">
For a brand-new solution, the biggest difference between the quickstarts is the language of the generated AppHost. Choose the page that matches how you want to author the orchestration layer.
</Aside>
