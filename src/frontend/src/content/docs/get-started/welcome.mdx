## Get started

Use the following steps to get started with Aspire:

<Steps>
1. [Check prerequisites](/get-started/prerequisites/): Ensure you have the required tools and SDKs installed.
1. [Install Aspire](/get-started/install-cli/): Get the Aspire tooling set up on your development machine.
1. [Build your first app](/get-started/first-app/): Follow our quickstart guide to create your first Aspire application.
</Steps>
